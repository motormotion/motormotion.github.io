About Us

Motormotion is a family owned and operated business that began in 2000 as a parts supplier and reconditioner operating from home. In 2004 the name A.A. Exchange Engines and Gearboxes was registered and the business began running out of a small commercial premises. Since then, we have grown and evolved, eventually expanding to incorporate Alice Auto Wreckers in 2007. Today, Motormotion comprises two warehouses on our premises as well as a storage yard that holds between 120 and 150 cars at any one time.

At Motormotion we strive to be the name behind the best quality parts and service in Alice Springs. We focus on dismantling mostly newer model cars and four-wheel-drives in good condition, ensuring you receive a part that is up to our rigorous standards. We also supply a huge range of new parts, from electrical components like window switches and alternators to drivetrain, suspension and engine components.

When you need parts that you can rely on in the outback, don't settle for anything less than a Motormotion part.
